Jean-Christophe Dubacq

Jean-Christophe Dubacq is maître de conférences (roughly Associate Professor) at LIPN for research and IUT of Villetaneuse (Technological Institute of Villetaneuse) for teaching. Both are part of the Paris 13 University, itself a member of the Sorbonne Paris Cité grouping of universities. Jean-Christophe Dubacq is currently head of the Computer Science department of the IUT.

You may find more information about his research. As he teaches only in French, please consider the French version of this site if you are interested in his lectures.

A French curriculum vitæ is available. Here is also a list in BibTeX format of his publications.
