Registration rates are 500 euros for regular attendees and 300 euros for doctoral students. The registration fee includes full housing and food at CIRM. Participants are expected to arrive Sunday evening on September 10 and to leave on September 15 in the afternoon.

Registrations will be reserved in priority to the authors of accepted papers that will be giving a talk.
