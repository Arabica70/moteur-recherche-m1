Here is a sketchy map (M=metro stations, IHP stands for Institut Henri Poincaré, where the conference holds). Note that the IHP is very near (=5 minute walk) from the metro (in fact "RER B") station "Luxembourg" (direct line to the Roissy=Charles de Gaulle airport). Most of the hotels are around, in this part of Paris which is called "Quarter Latin". Some of the (cheap) hotels are around the metro stations "Place d'Italie" or "Tolbiac". From there, you can take line 5 to "Place Monge", and then walk to the conference building (should be 5 minutes metro + 5 minute walk). If you need advices, don't hesitate to send us a mail.
